# Zepto Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This is analysis of **Zepto’s 10-minute grocery delivery operations**. The analysis, visualized through a multi-page interactive dashboard, evaluates key performance indicators (KPIs) across sales, customer demographics, product inventory, and transactional efficiency to provide actionable business insights.

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Sales Performance
* **Total Revenue:** ₹36.78M
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** ₹2.45K
* **Discount Impact:** Tracked total discounts (₹245K) against influencer-driven revenue (₹1.66M).
* **Key Visuals:** * Sales distribution by brand (Dabur, Britannia, Amul, Parle, Nestle, Haldirams).
    * Weekday sales trends (Wednesday peaking at ₹6.16M).
    * Comparison of Monthly Sales vs. Sales After Discount.

### 2. Customer Insights
* **Demographics:** Analyzes a customer base of 6,340 across 90 cities, with an average customer age of ~44 years.
* **Retention:** Tracks the "Repeat Purchase Rate" by state, identifying high-loyalty regions like Odisha and Jharkhand.
* **Ordering Patterns:** Breakdown of orders by time of day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night).

### 3. Product Analysis
* **Inventory Stats:** Oversight of 6,266 unique products.
* **Top Performers:** Identification of high-revenue products such as *Earum Juice* and *Quo Milk*.
* **Inventory Health:** Monitoring "Slow Moving Products" (e.g., *Itaque Soap*, *Optio Juice*) to optimize stock turnover.
* **Growth Metrics:** Month-over-Month (MOM) sales growth tracking, featuring a significant peak of 16.4% in March.

### 4. Transactions & Logistics
* **Reliability:** Monitoring success vs. failure rates for over 1,900 successful transactions.
* **Regional Success:** Analysis of successful transaction amounts by state (UP, Odisha, Tamil Nadu, etc.).
* **Payment Gateways:** Volume breakdown across UPI, Wallet, Credit/Debit Cards, and COD.
* **Partner Performance:** Rating tracking for delivery partners including Blue Dart, Delhivery, and Ecom Express.

## Key Insights
* **Geographic Leaders:** Cities like Tezpur, Jalandhar, and Jamshedpur lead in sales volume.
* **Brand Dominance:** Dabur and Britannia emerge as the highest-grossing brands.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Delivery partners maintain a consistent average rating of 3.03, suggesting a stable but improvable logistics framework.

## Technologies Used
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI
* **Data Source:** Zepto Operational Data (2023 - 2025)
* **Format:** PDF Export / Interactive Dashboard
